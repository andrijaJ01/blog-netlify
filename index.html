My Linux Rice

by Andrija JovanovicApril 23th 2021

By the end of this You'll have the same configuration as me, possibly with knowledge as well to adapt it to your needs. Main feature of this configuration are dynamic colors for everything based on wallpaper. 

With this setup I use Emacs with xresources theme, pywal for setting wallpaper and generating themes, rofi as menu switcher. I am by no means expert so if any more experienced people read this and find something that could be done more efficiently please let me know, I am always happy to learn.

Custom Firefox Homepage

This Configuration will feature custom new tab page that will have cards with links, current time on bottom and several search engines to choose from: DuckDuckGo, Google, Reddit, Youtube. 

Conclusion

After this series of articles you'll hopefully have basic understanding of how to customize your linux to suit your needs and you'll have my configuration as jump start to get there faster as begginer.
